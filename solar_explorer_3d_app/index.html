Explore the Solar System

This project presents three individual celestial models and three system views to explain how the Sun, Earth and Moon interact.

Sun

View the Sun as a glowing 3D model. It acts as the central light source in the solar system scene.

Earth

Explore the Earth model with a realistic surface texture showing oceans, continents, deserts and polar regions.

Moon

Sun–Earth System

This system view explains how the Earth's axial tilt and revolution around the Sun are the main reasons for the four seasons.

Earth–Moon System

This system view explains how the Moon orbits the Earth and why people observe different moon phases from Earth.
